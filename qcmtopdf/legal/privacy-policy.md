# Privacy Policy - Qcm To Pdf

**Effective Date:** [Insert Date]

Thank you for using **Qcm To Pdf** (the "Application"). This privacy policy explains how your
information is handled when you use our app.

## Information Collection and Use

**Qcm To Pdf** does not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use its
features.

However, the Application uses **Firebase Analytics**, a service provided by Google LLC, to collect
anonymous usage data. This data helps us understand how the app is used and improve its stability and
performance. The types of information collected by Firebase Analytics may include:

- Device information (such as model, operating system version, unique identifiers)
- App usage data (screens visited, actions performed, session duration)
- General location information (such as country)

This information does **not** allow us to personally identify you.

## File Handling

When you convert QCM files into PDF, the files remain on your device. **Qcm To Pdf** does not upload
or share your files with us or third parties.

## Data Sharing

We do **not** share your personal information or files with any third parties, except as required by
law.

Firebase Analytics data may be shared with Google in accordance with their privacy policy. For more
information about how Google handles your data, please see:

[Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage
is 100% secure.

## Your Choices

If you prefer not to share analytics data, you may disable analytics collection in your device
settings (e.g., via Google Play Services settings).

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by
updating this page.
