The Firebase analytics tools collect information that assists us in improving the service. We collect

## 3. App Created Data and Quiz

All the information you fill when creating a quiz or when filling your quiz author profile are used
for the creation of your questionnaire file and also for signing your editing in order to link your
creation to its legitimate author. This is essentially at your request.

Of course, you can choose to not fill your author profile in app (name or pseudo, email,
bibliography, etc.). However, by doing this, you should assume that your quiz will be marked with the
mention "Anonymous author".

All your created quizzes are stored locally under your storage drive and are never saved on our
server.

However, you have the ability to export your created quiz as simple portable file and export it
anywhere you want, even on external sites. Nevertheless, note that these external sites are not
necessarily operated by us. Therefore, we strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy of these
websites. We have no control over, and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or
